



# 📊 Atliq Hardware: Business Insights 360 (End-to-End Analysis)

## 📌 Project Overview
Atliq Hardware, a global consumer electronics leader, achieved a staggering **303% Net Sales growth**, yet faced a critical **-11.64% Net Profit margin**. This project provides a 360-degree diagnostic of the company’s Finance, Sales, Marketing, and Supply Chain operations to identify the root causes of this "Growth Trap."

## 🔍 Deep-Dive Analysis by View

### 1️⃣ Finance View: The Profitability Gap
* **Observation:** Net Sales surged from $29M to $3.7B, but Net Profit % declined by **-157%**.
* **Insight:** Gross Margin remains healthy at **38%**, proving that the product itself is profitable. However, high Operational Expenses (Ads, Promos, Placement Fees) are scaling faster than revenue.
* **Recommendation:** Shift focus from "Volume at any cost" to "Profitable Growth" by capping promotional spending.

### 2️⃣ Customer & Market View: The India Paradox
This view uses a **Performance Matrix (4-Quadrant Analysis)** to categorize 27 market zones and 74 customers based on Avg Net Sales vs. Avg Gross Margin %.

* **The India Sink:** India is the "Volume King" ($1.27B Sales) but operates at a **-22.03% Net Profit**. We are effectively "buying" market share at a loss.
* **Strong Zones:** USA, Canada, and China are the primary "Strong" markets where volume and margin are balanced.
* **Customer Risk:** Only **15 out of 74 customers** are in the "Strong" quadrant. High dependency on retailers (89% of sales) is eroding margins via heavy placement fees.

### 3️⃣ Product View: The SKU Problem
* **The 4% Rule:** Only **17 out of 397 products** are "Stars" (Strong Sales & Margin).
* **Volume Trap:** 96% of the portfolio (380 products) sits in the "Volume" quadrant, requiring heavy discounting to move.
* **Top Products:** AQ HOME Allin1 Gen 2 and AQ BZ Allin1 Gen 2 are the core revenue engines.

### 4️⃣ Supply Chain View: The Root Cause
The "smoking gun" of the financial loss lies in supply chain inefficiencies.
* **Forecasting Failure:** While accuracy is 80%, **Net Error surged by +129%**.
* **Stockout Loss:** Atliq lost **$318.85M** in potential revenue because it didn't have stock for high-demand items like **Processors**.
* **Overstock Drain:** Simultaneously, **$43.66M** was wasted on inventory costs for products that aren't selling.

## 💡 Final Strategic Roadmap
| Quadrant | Strategic Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Strong (Stars)** | **Protect:** Build loyalty and reinvest in R&D. |
| **Growth Opportunity** | **Invest:** Increase marketing spend in high-margin regions (USA/Canada). |
| **Margin Improvement** | **Optimize:** Cut discounts and improve logistics efficiency in India. |
| **At Risk** | **Rationalize:** Discontinue the bottom 20% of underperforming SKUs to reduce overstock costs. |
